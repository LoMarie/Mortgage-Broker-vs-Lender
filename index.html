Mortgage Broker
 
  • Helps customers identify the best lender for the situation
  • Helps customers compare a variety of loans from different lenders
  • Brings borrowers and mortgage lenders together by acting as the middle man
  • Do not use own to funds to advance mortgage loans
  • Communicate between borrowers and lenders during the application and approval process
  • One Stop Shop – no need to visit multiple lenders for the best rate
  • Shows all options but also has to disclose all options
  • Compensation capped at 2.75%
  • Responsible for all aspects of business, including payroll, compliance, taxes, sending MCR and bonding
 
Direct Lender with MiMutual
 
  • Decides whether person qualifies for the loan and then provides them the funding for the mortgage
  • Financial institution that approves and finances mortgage loans
  • Lender and borrower communicate directly with each other
  • More flexibility – do not need someone else’s permission to give the money
  • Can provide more competitive rate
  • Has a team that is responsible for the business so you can focus on selling alone
  • Compensation is not capped, can receive SRP & Points
